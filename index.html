About Jason Tuttle

I'm Jason Tuttle, a proud Republican resident of Lenoir City, TN. I've been serving our community since 2005, working in various capacities, including roles tied to public safety and local governance. My background in law enforcement and public service has equipped me to advocate for controlled growth, transparency, accountability, and sustainability in Loudon County.

Currently, I work at the Dollar Tree right next to Walmart in Lenoir City. This keeps me grounded and accessible—stop by anytime to chat about your concerns or how I can serve you on the County Commission. I'm running for the 2nd District Seat to bring real change and ensure our county grows responsibly.

With nearly two decades of community service, I understand the challenges facing our district and have the experience to address them effectively. Together, we can build a stronger, more transparent Loudon County.

Where I Stand

✓
Support Controlled Growth

Ensuring development serves our community's long-term interests while preserving our rural character and quality of life.

✓
Implement BidNet Countywide

Bringing transparency to government contracts and ensuring taxpayer dollars are spent efficiently through competitive bidding.

✓
Prioritize Transparency & Accountability

Making government decisions open to public scrutiny and holding elected officials accountable to the people they serve.

✓
Focus on Sustainability

Balancing economic development with environmental stewardship to preserve our natural resources for future generations.

✗
Raise Taxes Unnecessarily

I believe in responsible fiscal management and will fight against tax increases that burden hardworking families.

✗
Allow Unchecked Development

Development must be planned, sustainable, and benefit our existing community rather than overwhelming our infrastructure.
