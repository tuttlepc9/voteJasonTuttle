Meet Jason: Committed, Conservative, Community-First

I'm Jason Tuttle, a lifelong Lenoir City Republican with deep ties to Loudon County. With 20 years in public safety, broadband innovation, and community service, I'm ready to deliver conservative solutions for our growing county. From leading rural broadband expansion at LCUB to serving as a Housing Authority Commissioner, I've tackled real challenges with results.

With a Doctor of Business Administration (Walden University, 4.0 GPA), a Master's in Information Systems Management, and certifications like Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE), I bring data-driven solutions to county challenges. My research on broadband expansion and cybersecurity shows my commitment to progress grounded in practical results.

As a Republican, I stand for limited government and putting our community first. I'm not a politician - I'm a problem solver who believes in listening to residents and delivering real solutions. Let's keep Loudon County thriving while protecting what makes it special.

Conservative Solutions for Loudon County

My platform reflects Republican values and your priorities in District 2

Controlled Growth

Push for smart zoning to manage growth while preserving our rural charm. Ensure new developments support existing infrastructure. Population growth demands planning to avoid overcrowded schools and roads.

Transparency

Use tools like BidNet for fair bidding and make budgets fully public. Taxpayers deserve clarity after recent school funding and grant debates. Every dollar spent should be visible and justified.

Fiscal Responsibility

Block unnecessary tax hikes and focus spending on public safety, schools, and infrastructure. Families need relief, not higher taxes, in today's economy.

Safety & Sustainability

Fund law enforcement and emergency services while protecting our water and land. Growth requires safe communities and sustainable resources for the future.

Infrastructure First

With new housing and businesses booming, I'll ensure development doesn't outpace our roads, schools, or water systems. My LCUB broadband work proved I can balance progress with practicality.

Economic Relief

No tax hikes—my budget priorities will fund essentials like public safety and drought solutions without waste. Your family budget matters, and so should the county's.
